Green tea is made by preventing oxidation, unlike black teas. The tea leaves are quickly dried after being picked. This prevents oxidation. These teas are usually steeped for shorter periods of time and at lower temperatures. Green tea usually has less caffiene than other teas. The tea shown above is matcha tea, which is made by taking young tea leaves and grinding them into a fine powder. One of my favourite green teas (along with matcha) is jasmine green tea.
