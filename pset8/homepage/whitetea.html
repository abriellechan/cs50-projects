White teas are one of the most simple teas to make. The leaves are just gently dried right after picking. It is handled minimally until the leaves are dry, so no rolling or further proccessing. The flavour of white tea is very light, and it is hard for the tea to get bitter. This tea is not oxidized very much. The image above shows the white tea that was chopped and put into a teabag. I drank all my whole leaf white tea.
